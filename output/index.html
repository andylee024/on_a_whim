About the Author

Hello. My name is Andy. I wear a few hats. I am currently a motion planning engineer at Uber ATG (Advanced Technologies Group), Uber’s R&D division for self-driving cars. I am a former startup founder of Ikigai, a computational drug-discovery startup focused on Alzheimer’s (accepted to YCombinator Summer 2018). I also formerly collaborated with the Neurogenomics Laboratory, a computational biology lab using machine learning to identify epigenetic drug targets for Alzheimer’s.

On a Whim

I care about technology that impacts the world in a profoundly positive way and the process by which the world adopts that technology. This blog is named “On a Whim” because I believe transformative technology is started by scientists, entrepreneurs, dreamers who pursue an idea on a whim. To that end, “On a Whim” is my vehicle for communicating thoughts on two of my passions: math and entrepreneurship.
